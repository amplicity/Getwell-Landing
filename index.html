The new healthcare standard

The Problem

The healthcare system is vast and complex, but the software applications that doctors use should not be. A new study recently revealed that physicians feel more hindered than helped by their Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems.

When it comes to medical data, there is no standard. Moving records from system to system is tedious, expensive, and timely. How did such an integral part of our society become so unorganized, confusing and disjointed?









Our Solution

We've revolutionized the EHR experience.

What about other EHR systems, in other practices of medicine? How could we help their users operate more efficiently?











Getwell aggregates treatment plan data to provide crowd-sourced treatment recommendations

Getwell is a web application that can be used by any doctor, with any web-based EHR. It takes less than one minute to setup, and one click to access real-time treatment options based on diagnosis information.

Getwell uses anonymous data and is completely HIPAA compliant.
