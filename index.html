The new healthcare standard






The Problem

The healthcare system is vast and complex, but the software applications that doctors use should not be. A new study recently revealed that physicians feel more hindered than helped by their Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems.

When it comes to medical data, there is no standard. Moving records from system to system is tedious, expensive, and timely. How did such an integral part of our society become so unorganized, confusing and disjointed?









Our Solution

We've revolutionized the EHR experience from the ground up through crowdsourcing, macros, and more efficient workflows.

Getwell is an EHR we've intially optimized for optometry, it will be scaled to include other specialties in 2015.










Getwell is the most advanced EHR on the planet



We will cut the time doctors spend on their computers by 50%. This is accomplished through over 20 years of workflow design. The Getwell team is lead by Dr. Anthony Diecidue, OD.

Getwell is powered by the latest web technologies to ensure a fast, reliable, multi-platform experience. We are available on every device.
